Privacy summary

Offline-first by default, transparent when AI is enabled.

LT Smart Money: Budget & Finance stores manually entered financial records locally on the device. The optional Gemini Advisor is disabled by default. If you enable AI and submit a question, the app may send your prompt and selected finance context to Google's Gemini API over HTTPS so the advisor can respond.

  • No account creation is required for standard app use.
  • No location, contacts, camera, SMS, call log, or health permissions are requested.
  • AI redaction is enabled by default before Gemini requests are sent.
  • Users can export CSV data and clear locally stored app data.

Local finance records

Accounts, transactions, budgets, goals, debts, bills, and subscriptions are stored in the app's local database.

Optional Gemini processing

AI questions are user-initiated. When enabled, Gemini requests may include redacted financial context needed to answer the question.

Educational use only

The advisor is not a financial planner, tax advisor, investment advisor, lender, or legal advisor.

Important limitations

Use LT Smart Money as a personal tracking tool, not regulated financial advice.

No banking automation

LT Smart Money is designed around user-entered records and planning views. It does not move money or replace a bank.

No professional advice

AI output and app calculations are educational estimates. Seek qualified professional advice for financial, tax, credit, lending, legal, or investment decisions.

User-controlled exports

CSV exports may contain sensitive financial data. Share exported files only with apps and people you trust.
