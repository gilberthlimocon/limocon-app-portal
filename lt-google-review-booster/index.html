QR placement playbook

Print the QR once. Put it where customers already look.

The best review request is visible, polite, and easy to act on. Place the QR at natural pause points: checkout counters, bill clippers, information desks, reception tables, service counters, and thank-you cards.

  • Use short, neutral copy: "Your honest review helps our local business."
  • Train staff to mention the QR only after service is complete.
  • Keep the same QR link across print, SMS, and direct share workflows.
