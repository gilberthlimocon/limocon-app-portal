LT Google Review Booster

Support and legal resources

LT Google Review Booster helps business owners create non-gated Google review request links through QR, share-sheet, and eligible paid SMS workflows.

Account access may use email/password, Google sign-in, or Apple sign-in when configured. Account deletion is handled directly in the app through Settings - Delete account and data. The deletion help page is available only as a support reference.
