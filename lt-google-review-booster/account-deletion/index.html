LT Google Review Booster Account Deletion

Effective date: July 12, 2026

If you created an LT Google Review Booster account, delete your account and app-owned data from inside the app. The deletion workflow requires re-authentication before the final confirmation step.

Self-Service Account Deletion

Open LT Google Review Booster, sign in, go to Settings, and choose Delete account and data. The app will ask you to re-authenticate with your password, Google sign-in, or Apple sign-in depending on how you access your account.

After re-authentication, the app shows a final irreversible deletion warning. If you confirm deletion, LT Google Review Booster deletes your account and app-owned data automatically.

If You Cannot Access The App

If you cannot sign in or complete the in-app deletion workflow, email support.reviews@limocontechnologies.com with the subject "LT Google Review Booster Account Deletion" to request account deletion.

Include the email address used for your LT Google Review Booster account and the business name configured in the app. Do not include customer phone numbers, passwords, payment card numbers, or other unnecessary sensitive information.

What We Delete

When account deletion is confirmed in the app, we delete or de-identify account data and associated business data unless we need to retain certain records for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, tax, dispute, app-store, or messaging compliance reasons.

What May Be Retained

We may retain records required to honor SMS opt-outs, document subscription/billing history, prevent fraud or abuse, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or complete app-store and messaging compliance requirements.

Subscriptions

Deleting your LT Google Review Booster account does not automatically cancel an active Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. To stop future billing, cancel the subscription in your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings.
