LT Google Review Booster Account Deletion

Effective date: July 12, 2026

If you created an LT Google Review Booster account, you can request account and data deletion from inside the app or by contacting Limocon Technologies.

Request Deletion In The App

Open LT Google Review Booster, sign in, go to Settings, and choose Request account deletion. The app records a deletion request for your authenticated account.

Request Deletion By Email

Email support.reviews@limocontechnologies.com with the subject "LT Google Review Booster Account Deletion".

Include the email address used for your LT Google Review Booster account and the business name configured in the app. Do not include customer phone numbers, passwords, payment card numbers, or other unnecessary sensitive information.

What We Delete

When we process a deletion request, we delete or de-identify account data and associated business data unless we need to retain certain records for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, tax, dispute, app-store, or messaging compliance reasons.

What May Be Retained

We may retain records required to honor SMS opt-outs, document subscription/billing history, prevent fraud or abuse, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or complete app-store and messaging compliance requirements.

Subscriptions

Deleting your LT Google Review Booster account does not automatically cancel an active Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. To stop future billing, cancel the subscription in your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings.

Contact

For account deletion help, contact support.reviews@limocontechnologies.com.
