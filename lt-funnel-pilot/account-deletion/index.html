LT Funnel Pilot Account Deletion

Pre-launch draft — pending legal review

If you created an LT Funnel Pilot account, you can delete your account and app-owned data from inside the app. The deletion workflow requires re-authentication before the final confirmation step.

Self-Service Account Deletion

Open LT Funnel Pilot, sign in, go to Settings, and choose the delete account option. The app will ask you to re-authenticate. After re-authentication, the app shows a final irreversible deletion warning. If you confirm deletion, LT Funnel Pilot deletes your account and app-owned data.

Export Your Data First

Deleting your account may also remove your workspaces, published funnel pages, and the lead data held in those workspaces. Export any leads, content, or records you need before you delete your account, because deletion cannot be undone.

If You Cannot Access The App

If you cannot sign in or complete the in-app deletion workflow, email support.funnel@limocontechnologies.com with the subject "LT Funnel Pilot Account Deletion" to request account deletion.

Include the email address used for your LT Funnel Pilot account and the workspace or business name configured in the app. Do not include lead phone numbers, passwords, payment card numbers, or other unnecessary sensitive information.

What We Delete

When account deletion is confirmed, we delete or de-identify account and workspace data and associated business, funnel, and lead data unless we need to retain certain records for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, tax, dispute, app-store, or messaging-compliance reasons.

What May Be Retained

We may retain records required to honor messaging opt-outs and suppression requests, document subscription and billing history, prevent fraud or abuse, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or complete app-store and messaging-compliance requirements.

Subscriptions

Deleting your LT Funnel Pilot account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. To stop future billing, cancel your subscription through the cancellation method for the way you subscribed, including your app store account settings if you subscribed through an app store.
