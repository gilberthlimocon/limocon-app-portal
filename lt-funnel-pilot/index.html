LT Funnel Pilot is a mobile-first AI funnel workspace for business owners, marketing service providers, and funnel builders. It turns a completed business profile into a published lead-generation funnel with AI-generated brand positioning, landing pages, sales pages, email and SMS drafts, lead capture, and a lightweight CRM.

The pages below are pre-launch drafts prepared during Phase 1 planning. They describe the product's intended behavior and must be reviewed by counsel and reconciled with the final build before production use.
