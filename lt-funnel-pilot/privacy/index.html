LT Funnel Pilot Privacy Policy

Pre-launch draft — pending legal review

LT Funnel Pilot helps business owners, marketing service providers, and funnel builders turn a business profile into a published lead-generation funnel. Account holders use the mobile app to generate brand, landing page, sales page, email, and SMS content with AI; publish public funnel pages; capture leads; and follow up. Members of the public do not install the app; they may view a published funnel page in a browser and submit a lead form.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who processes it for us, the difference between account data and lead data, and how users can delete their account and related app-owned data.

1. Our Two Roles

For account, business profile, generated content, and subscription information, Limocon Technologies acts as the controller of that information.

For lead information that account holders collect from the public through their published funnels, Limocon Technologies acts as a processor or service provider on behalf of the account holder. The account holder decides why leads are collected and how they are contacted, and is responsible for having a lawful basis and any required consent. Business customers may request a Data Processing Addendum and current sub-processor list from the contact address below.

2. Information We Collect

Account information may include name, email address, authentication identifiers, session information, and password data handled by our authentication provider.

Business and funnel information may include business name, category, description, location or service area, contact details, website and social links, offer and audience details, proof points, disclaimers, uploaded logo and brand images, brand profile, funnel content, page drafts, lead form configuration, and email and SMS drafts.

Lead information submitted by the public through a published funnel may include name, email address, phone number, form answers, consent selections, and non-identifying page-activity signals. Account holders control this information.

Usage and analytics information may include page views, CTA clicks, form starts, form submissions, publish events, and generation events. We exclude raw email addresses, phone numbers, notes, and message bodies from generic analytics event payloads.

Subscription information may include plan, entitlement status, billing state, and renewal or cancellation status received from our billing provider. We do not receive or store full payment card numbers.

3. AI Content Generation

When an account holder requests AI generation, we send the relevant business, brand, funnel, offer, or lead context to a third-party AI provider on our servers so the provider can return draft content. AI output is stored as an editable draft and is not published or sent to any recipient without an explicit account-holder action. We record generation metadata such as prompt key, prompt version, model, input snapshot, output snapshot, user, and timestamps.

4. Messaging Information For SMS And Email

In the initial release, live SMS and live email sending are disabled by default. The app generates SMS and email drafts that account holders can edit, copy, and export. Live sending is enabled only after provider, verified sender, recorded consent, billing entitlement, opt-out handling, and messaging-registration checks are complete.

When live sending is enabled, we process recipient contact details and consent records through a messaging provider only to deliver the messages the account holder sends, and we maintain opt-out and suppression records so recipients who opt out are not messaged again.

5. How We Use Information

We use information to create and manage accounts and workspaces, configure business and brand profiles, generate and store AI drafts, publish funnel pages, capture and manage leads, provide the CRM and analytics, process subscriptions and entitlements, enforce plan limits and safety gates, provide support, prevent fraud and abuse, moderate reported content, troubleshoot issues, and maintain security and legal compliance.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information, and we do not share lead information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We use service providers that process information for us so the app can function.

Current categories of service providers include our backend and database provider, our AI generation provider, our SMS provider, our email provider, our billing provider, and our hosting provider. A current sub-processor list is available to business customers on request.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, to prevent abuse or fraud, or as part of a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets involving Limocon Technologies.

7. Public Funnel Pages And Cookies

Published funnel pages are public web pages. When required, published pages present a consent choice before setting non-essential cookies or analytics, and account holders are responsible for the accuracy of the content and disclosures on their pages. Members of the public who submit a lead form provide their information directly to the account holder's workspace.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, comply with legal obligations, and protect the service. Account and workspace data is retained while the account is active. Lead records, opt-out records, subscription records, consent records, and deletion-request records are retained as needed for the account holder's use, billing support, fraud prevention, legal, tax, dispute, app-store, and messaging-compliance purposes.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information, including encryption in transit, backend access controls, row-level security policies, server-side secrets, and limiting sensitive operations such as AI generation, publishing, messaging, and account deletion to backend functions. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Choices And Rights

Account holders may update business and funnel information in the app, unpublish funnels, stop using messaging features, cancel subscriptions, and delete their account and app-owned data through the app.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your information, and to opt out of certain processing. For lead information, we act on the account holder's instructions; members of the public who submitted a lead form should contact the business that operates the funnel, and we will assist that business in honoring valid requests. To exercise rights over your own account information, contact us at the address below.

11. Account And Data Deletion

If you created an LT Funnel Pilot account, delete your account in the app through Settings. If you cannot access the app, use the web resource at Account Deletion to request deletion.

Before deletion, the app may require recent authentication. After confirmation, app-owned account and workspace data are deleted or de-identified unless we need to retain certain records for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, tax, dispute, or messaging compliance reasons. Deleting your account may also remove published funnel pages and associated lead data held in your workspace; export any data you need first. If you purchased a subscription, cancel it to stop future billing.

12. Children

LT Funnel Pilot is intended for business use and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Account holders are responsible for not collecting children's information through their funnels without a lawful basis.

13. International Use

Information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. Where information about individuals in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom is processed, we use an appropriate transfer mechanism and offer business customers a Data Processing Addendum with standard contractual clauses.

14. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and may provide additional notice in the app or on our website.

15. Contact

For privacy questions, support, data-processing requests, or account deletion requests when you cannot access the app, contact Limocon Technologies at support.funnel@limocontechnologies.com.
